Plant Care ID

Full description

Sedum morganianum is an evergreen trailing succulent with many over 2 feet (60 cm) long or more, pendent stems arising from base. Fleshy blue-green leaves are crowded along stems and up to 0.5 inch (1.3 cm) long.

Dimensions

10"d x 12"h

Difficulty

Easy

Sun

Direct Sun Light

Water

Water every 2 weeks when soil is completely dry
